Lottery Smart Contract

This Lottery Smart Contract involves a simple lottery system in with the users can enter into the lottery by paying a minimum of 0.1 ether to the lottery contract.Any one who can pay a minimum of 0.1 ether can enter into the lottery.The manger (i.e the person who deployed the contract) will pick up the winner of the lottery.Since the Solidity doesn’t have a random number generator library a pseudo number generator has been defined to pick a random number to select the winner.The total lottery amount will be transferred to the winner.
